About Me

I am a PhD student in Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of Alabama, specializing in electric machine design and control. My research focuses on developing rare-earth-free permanent magnet motors for electric vehicles and wind turbines.

As a Graduate Research Assistant at the Renewable Energy System Laboratory (RESyL) , I conduct FEM-based simulations using ANSYS Maxwell and MotorCAD to analyze torque-speed performance, efficiency maps, and rare-earth-free magnet alternatives.

Previously, I contributed to soil moisture and vegetation detection using multispectral imaging at the UA Remote Sensing Centre. I also have teaching and industry experience as a lecturer and automation engineer.

Research Projects

Rare-Earth-Free PM Motor Design for EVs

Design and optimization of non-rare-earth PMSMs using FEM simulations, benchmarking against commercial EV traction motors.

ANSYS Maxwell MotorCAD EV Motors

FEM Simulations of PMSM Variants

Conducting ANSYS Maxwell & MotorCAD simulations of SPM, IPM, SynRM, and PMSynRM motors to evaluate torque, efficiency, and demagnetization tradeoffs.

Simulation SPM/IPM MotorCAD

Magnetic Circuit & Demagnetization Studies

Investigating PM coercivity, recoil line dynamics, and demagnetization effects under armature MMF to enhance durability and performance of PMSM designs.

Magnetics MMF Reliability

Nissan Leaf PMSM Benchmarking

Research Presentations

Performance of Rare-Earth-Free PMASynR Motor for Electric Vehicles: Discovered Fe₂Ni₂N Magnets

This poster presented FEM-based analysis of a PMASynR motor using Fe₂Ni₂N magnets, a rare-earth-free alternative. The study demonstrated competitive performance for EV applications while reducing reliance on rare-earth materials.

Presented by: Sayem Ul Alam, 2024

A Convolutional Neural Network Approach to Soil Moisture Mapping Using Multispectral UAS Imagery

We developed a CNN-based approach for estimating soil moisture using multispectral UAS imagery. The method enhances agricultural monitoring and supports precision farming through accurate soil moisture mapping.

Presented by: Sayem Ul Alam, 2023
